Monstera Plant - Your New Green Companion

Everything you need to know about caring for your Monstera. From light needs to repotting tips.

Watch: Monstera Growth Stages and Care Routine

Plant Details

  • Size: 12 inches (medium) / 24 inches (large)
  • Light Requirements: Bright, indirect light
  • Watering Requirements: Water when the top 1-2 inches of soil are dry
  • Toxicity: Mildly toxic to pets (cats & dogs) - keep out of reach of pets
  • Repotting Recommendations: Repot every 1-2 years as it grows
